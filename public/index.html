About us

Welcome to the homepage of the AI and Machine Learning Club at Cal High, we are a club who aims to reduce the stigma behind Data Structures at a high-school level. Our Mission Statement: educate fellow high school students about the growing industry of data science and, more specifically, the applications of data structures.

Join us by entering the discord server!

We want to welcome you on the journey to become a better computer scientists that innovates and helps society in multiple ways. We provide the resources and help but the imagination, creativity and determination must come from you. We will support yout decisions and help you when in need.
